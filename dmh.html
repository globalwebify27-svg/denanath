About DMH

Deenanath Mangeshkar Hospital and Research Center is a charitable, multi-specialty hospital located in the heart of Pune, India. Founded in 2001, today it is one of the largest hospital in Pune, with 800 beds. Deenanath Mangeshkar Hospital and Research Center offers state-of-the-art diagnostic, therapeutic and intensive care facilities in a one-stop medical center.
