2) Lupus Day Programme: Patient Education and Awareness

Rheumatology department Deenanath hospital and WHIRA celebrated world lupus day on 1 May 2024 at Deenanath hospital. It was organized and conducted by Dr. Vaijayanti Lagujoshi.

The programme involved Dr. Manjiri Dixit (Consultant Psychiatrist), Dr. Varsha Jagtap (Endocrinologist) and Dr. Kavita Krishna (Rheumatologist) as guest speakers who delivered lectures on lupus related topics.

The programme also involved patient felicitation and experience sharing. It was very well attended and appreciated by 70 people.
