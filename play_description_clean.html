Dept. Of Shoulder & Sports Injuries

Welcome to the “Department of Shoulder & Sports Injuries”. The Shoulder unit was formed as an independent unit from the Orthopaedic department in August 2009. The increased workload & necessity to integrate allied services compelled us to create this Department.

Department Location & Timings

The OPD & department are situated on the 6th floor “D” wing of the main hospital building. Our Shoulder physiotherapist is available to give a one-to-one session to our patients from 8:30 AM to 5:00 PM.

Now the Shoulder physiotherapy PSRP programme is conducted next to the department OPD all day long. Shoulder USG is performed within the department on the 6th Floor by our Musculo-Skeletal USG specialist in no time with the report handed over instantly. In short, this is an all integrated department which provides a one stop solution.

Shoulder Care & Rehabilitation

Shoulder is a complex joint often defying medical treatment. Arguably we are the busiest department in the country performing the highest number of shoulder surgeries. We pride ourselves on a highly successful rehab programme which we have designed called PSRP - Pune Shoulder Rehab Programme.

What We Avoid

We do not believe in Gadgets or modalities like SWD or IFT. Even painkillers are not prescribed.

Our Approach

We believe in relieving pain with the help of a scientifically designed exercise programme with the use of an exercise band.
